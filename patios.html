Finish Options

Pick the Look That Fits Your Home

Every patio we pour can be finished to your taste. Here are the most popular options for Wyoming homes.

〰️

Broom Finish

The most common finish — a textured surface created by dragging a broom across the wet concrete. Slip-resistant, clean-looking, and pairs well with any home style. The practical standard for Wyoming patios.

🪨

Exposed Aggregate

The top layer of cement paste is washed away to reveal the natural stone aggregate beneath. A beautiful, textured surface with excellent traction that looks great against Wyoming's natural landscape.

✨

Smooth / Trowel Finish

A hand-troweled smooth surface that's clean and modern. Popular for covered patios and spaces where you'll use outdoor rugs or furniture. Not recommended for uncovered surfaces in wet conditions.

Why Concrete

The Long-Term Choice for Wyoming Patios

Wood decks require ongoing maintenance — staining, sealing, replacing boards — and they don't survive Wyoming winters as well as concrete. Pavers shift, settle, and grow weeds between joints over time. Concrete is a one-time investment that lasts for decades.

✓
30–50 year lifespan with minimal maintenance
✓
No rotting, warping, or splintering like wood decks
✓
No shifting or weed growth between joints like pavers
✓
Easy to clean — sweep or rinse with a hose
✓
Handles heavy outdoor furniture and fire pits without damage
✓
Adds real value to your property
